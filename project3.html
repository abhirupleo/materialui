What's a hackathon?

A Hackathon is a collaboration of programmers and designers to code, design and build innovative solutions to real life problems, through extensive coding in a short time duration. Hackathons are great way to promote innovation among students by bringing in cutting-edge problem statements. See this for a good explanation.

Your only constraint is: You only have 24 hours.

NIT Surat's foremost Hackathon.

Hackathons provide a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology. People with technical backgrounds come together, form teams around a problem or idea, and collaboratively code a unique solution from scratch - these generally take shape in the form of websites, mobile apps, and robots. So prepare yourself for a weekend filled with learning new technologies, building amazing products and making new friends all within the span of 24 hours.
